Orton-Gillingham

Orton-Gillingham Tutoring

Orton-Gillingham is a multi-sensory method for teaching reading and spelling skills to students with dyslexia. Our Orton-Gillingham-based program begins with basic phonemic awareness skills and progresses to advanced roots and foreign language influence. Our program is built directly from The Orton-Gillingham Method developed by Anna Gillingham, Betsy Stillman, and Samuel Orton

Students will learn to break words into phonemes (the smallest sounds in language), divide multi-syllable words, identify prefixes and suffixes, and learn about reading and spelling rules to help them better decode written words, break down spelling words, and increase understanding.

After completing the Orton-Gillingham program, students can advance to learning Grammar, Language Arts, Reading Copmrehension, and Writing Skills.
