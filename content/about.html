Information

The first session with your tutor will be an introductory or evaluation session. Introductory sessions can range from 15 minutes to 1 hour depending on the duration of evaluation, questions you may have, and tutor availability. During this session, you will be able to share your learning objectives and ask questions about the program.

All sessions are conducted through Zoom's online meeting platform where you can meet with your tutor one-on-one or in a group session. If you are unable to access Zoom for any reason, please let us know and we will try to accommodate you with a different software.

Before your first session, your tutor will send you a scheduled or recurring link to join your meeting depending on how many sessions you plan to complete with your tutor. You may also recieve a calendar invite containing this link if it is compatible with your email provider.

There will be no fee for missed or rescheduled lessons. You may lose your reserved time slot if you are absent for more than two classes in a row without contacting your tutor. If you wish to reschedule, please contact your tutor to coordinate a time to meet.

An invoice will be sent after each stand-alone session with a link to pay. For recurring classes, an invoice with a link to pay will be sent at the beginning (if you choose to purchase lesson credits in advance) or end of each month.

For other questions, please email us at learntodecode@gmail.com
