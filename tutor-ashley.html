Patient and Knowledgeable Tutor with 5+ Years of Experience

Hi! I'm Ashley. I have been tutoring for over 5 years and have experience working with students of all ages and abilities, from preschoolers to professionals. I have tutored students studying English as a second language, as well as students with dyslexia, ADHD, autism, dysgraphia, and dyscalculia. I work to create a positive learning environment where my students can sharpen their strengths while improving any weaker areas.

Orton-Gillingham: I have been tutoring Orton-Gillingham for over 3 years and am working towards my certification. I specialize in reading and spelling for students with dyslexia. By applying the Orton-Gillingham reading and spelling method, I have helped several students improve their reading and spelling skills, as well as comprehension.

English: ELA, Grammar, Writing, and Reading Comprehension. I've been teaching Language Arts, Grammar, Writing, and Reading skills for over five years. As a 96th percentile scorer on the SAT Reading and Writing exams, I am well versed in comprehension strategies, grammar rules, and writing conventions. I'm happy to focus on one specific area (i.e. reading comprehension, grammar rules, writing skills, etc.) or teach general English Language skills depending on your tutoring needs.

ESL / Accent Reduction: I am a native American-English speaker with a strong background in English grammar and pronunciation. As a foreign language student, I understand common pronunciation errors made by ESL students, and exercises for gaining a natural American accent. I love learning about the history of languages and studying them. I currently study Mandarin Chinese, Russian, Spanish, and American Sign Language (ASL), as well as effective strategies to learn new languages.

Coding and Computer Skills: I started coding in 2014. I am proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap 5, and am also experienced with GitHub and web hosting. I teach front-end web development as well as JavaScript, jQuery, and basic Python skills, including basic JS game development. Math: I was a straight-A math student in high school and studied Algebra I, Algebra II/College Algebra, Trigonometry, Geometry, and Pre-Calculus.
